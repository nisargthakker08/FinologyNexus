Nexus Global Vendor Rigor Framework

Comprehensive Vendor Compliance Framework

Executive Summary

The Nexus Global Vendor Rigor Framework provides financial institutions with a comprehensive approach to vendor risk management across multiple jurisdictions. This framework addresses critical regulatory requirements while offering practical implementation guidance.

Global Compliance Coverage

EU, US, APAC regulatory frameworks including GDPR, PSD2, GLBA, SOX, HKMA, and RBI requirements.

Risk Assessment Tools

Standardized scoring methodology for vendor risk evaluation with clear RAG status indicators.

Documentation Templates

Ready-to-use templates for vendor agreements, due diligence checklists, and compliance reporting.

Implementation Roadmap

Step-by-step guidance for establishing a compliant vendor management program in 90 days.

Key Regulatory Areas Covered

European Union: GDPR data protection requirements, PSD2 payment services compliance, MiFID II financial instruments directives.

United States: GLBA financial privacy rules, SOX financial reporting requirements, FFIEC examination guidelines.

Asia-Pacific: HKMA supervisory policies, RBI outsourcing guidelines, data localization requirements across multiple jurisdictions.

Framework Components

Risk Assessment Matrix: Comprehensive evaluation criteria covering financial stability, operational resilience, compliance history, and cybersecurity posture.

Due Diligence Checklist: Step-by-step verification process for new vendor onboarding and periodic reviews.

Contract Management Guidelines: Essential clauses and requirements for vendor agreements across jurisdictions.

Monitoring & Reporting Framework: Ongoing oversight mechanisms and regulatory reporting templates.
