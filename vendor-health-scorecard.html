What the Scorecard Evaluates

Our comprehensive assessment covers four critical areas of vendor health, each with weighted scoring for accurate risk evaluation.

Financial Stability

Liquidity Assessment

Evaluate cash flow, working capital, and financial reserves to ensure operational continuity.

  • Current ratio analysis
  • Debt-to-equity assessment
  • Revenue concentration risk

Solvency Metrics

Assess long-term financial health and ability to meet obligations.

  • Profitability trends
  • Credit rating evaluation
  • Financial covenant compliance

Operational Resilience

Business Continuity

Evaluate disaster recovery capabilities and operational redundancy.

  • BCP testing frequency
  • Recovery time objectives
  • Alternate site availability

Staffing & Expertise

Assess human resource stability and technical capabilities.

  • Key personnel retention
  • Staff qualification levels
  • Succession planning

Compliance Alignment

Regulatory Adherence

Verify compliance with relevant financial regulations and standards.

  • Outsourcing guideline compliance
  • KYC/AML program effectiveness
  • Data protection alignment

Audit & Certification

Review audit history and relevant certifications.

  • Internal audit findings
  • External audit results
  • Industry certifications (ISO, SOC, etc.)

Cyber & Data Security

Security Framework

Evaluate information security policies and controls.

  • Security certification status
  • Access control mechanisms
  • Encryption standards

Incident Response

Assess breach detection and response capabilities.

  • Incident response plan maturity
  • Breach notification procedures
  • Security testing frequency
