How CashMap works

From your data
to a live forecast in days

01
Data Collection
We collect your current receivables schedule, payables commitments, payroll, and fixed costs. This happens via a structured intake form. No accounting system access required unless preferred.
02
Forecast Build
A Finology Nexus analyst builds your 12-week cash model in a format calibrated to your business structure. Not a template: built from your actual commitments and payment terms.
03
Risk Flag and Analysis
Shortfall risk dates are identified, working capital gaps are mapped, and scenario analyses are run for the decisions you told us you are considering. Each risk flag comes with a recommended response.
04

The model is delivered with a live walkthrough call. You leave knowing how to read it, how to update it, and exactly what needs your attention first. Ongoing support is available for businesses that want CashMap as a continuous service.
