What FinPulse measures

P&L and Profitability

Revenue trend assessment, gross and net margin health, cost structure analysis, and budget versus actual variance patterns. Margin gaps that are acceptable in SaaS may be critical in manufacturing. The scoring is calibrated to your industry.

  • Revenue growth trajectory over the past 12 months
  • Gross margin vs industry benchmarks for your sector
  • Net profitability and margin stability
  • Budget variance patterns indicating forecasting quality
üíß
Cash Flow and Liquidity

Working capital position, cash runway quantification, debtor days, and cash management maturity. Most businesses with a cash flow problem do not discover it in the numbers. They discover it in a bank balance. FinPulse finds it earlier.

  • Cash runway in months at current burn rate
  • Customer collection cycle and debtor days
  • Cash flow management approach and visibility
  • Working capital adequacy indicators

Risk and Exposure

Vendor concentration, cross-border financial exposure, FX risk, and repatriation complexity. Risks that feel manageable at the current scale can become critical within one funding cycle or one supplier failure.

  • Vendor and supplier concentration levels
  • Cross-border financial flows and FX exposure
  • Transfer pricing and repatriation complexity
  • Biggest current financial concern, weighted in scoring
‚öôÔ∏è
Operational Efficiency

Process maturity, financial function stability, and reporting quality. The structural indicators that predict whether a business will handle the next phase of growth or be caught by it. Efficiency gaps rarely cause problems today. They compound quietly and then arrive at once.

  • Financial process maturity and documentation
  • Reporting quality and decision-making support
  • Dependency on key individuals for financial execution
  • Audit and compliance readiness indicators

Common questions

What people ask
before running FinPulse

If you have a question not answered here, email us at hello@finologynexus.com and we will respond within one business day.

Is FinPulse really free? What is the catch?
Yes. The teaser report is free, takes 4 minutes, and requires no sign-up or payment information. The catch is that the full analysis: detailed recommendations, peer benchmarks, and a live call, requires a paid upgrade. The free version is a genuine diagnostic, not a marketing funnel disguised as a tool.
How accurate is an assessment based on 12 questions?
Accurate enough to identify the highest-impact issues and give you a reliable directional read. The questions are designed by senior financial analysts to capture the indicators most predictive of financial health at each business stage. It is not a full audit. It is a triage. The full report goes deeper.
What happens with my data?
Your responses are used only to generate your report. We do not store identifiable financial data beyond the session unless you opt into the full report engagement. No data is sold or shared with third parties. The assessment is designed to work without collecting sensitive financial documents.
Can I run FinPulse for a business outside India?
Yes. FinPulse is designed to work for any business globally. The scoring adjusts for country of operations and industry context. ComplianceCheck is the India-specific product; FinPulse covers financial health universally.
How does the full report differ from the teaser?
The teaser shows your score, dimension breakdown, and top 3 findings. The full report adds a detailed P&L analysis with benchmarks, a 13-week cash flow and runway model, 4 to 7 prioritised action recommendations in order, a vendor risk register, industry peer comparison, and a 30-minute live debrief call with a senior Finology Nexus analyst.
What if my answers change the score significantly?
You can retake FinPulse as often as you need. Many clients use it quarterly as a structured check-in against their financial position. Each run generates a fresh analysis based on your current inputs.
