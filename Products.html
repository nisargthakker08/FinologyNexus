Frequently Asked Questions

Are these products cloud-based?
We deliver reports and dashboards without forcing you into cloud. Early-stage customers get PDFs, secure links or Google Drive. Enterprise customers can opt for hosted integrations.
How much setup time is required?
Most products are usable within 3–7 business days. For dashboard integrations, allow 1–2 weeks for data mapping and permissions.
Do you support multiple jurisdictions (US, UK, EU, UAE)?
Yes. Our dataset templates and source checks include common international sources; we can adapt sources per jurisdiction as required.
Can we white-label the reports?
Yes — white-labeling and co-branded PDFs are available for Enterprise plans.
