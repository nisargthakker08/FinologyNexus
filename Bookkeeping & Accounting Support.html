Bookkeeping & Accounting Support

Accurate. Scalable. Reliable. Your back office, reimagined.

Finology Nexus empowers startups, accounting firms, and solo CPAs with dependable bookkeeping and accounting operations. From basic entries to full ledger maintenance, we ensure your books are clean, compliant, and ready for strategic decision-making.

What We Offer

• Daily, weekly, or monthly bookkeeping support
• Bank, credit card, and payment gateway reconciliations
• AP/AR tracking and invoicing
• Payroll accounting and journal entries
• Cleanup of historical books and tax-year support
• Support with QuickBooks, Xero, Zoho Books & more

Why Choose Us?

We act as an extension of your finance function — offering process efficiency, proactive communication, and data accuracy. Whether you're an early-stage founder, CFO, or a busy CPA, we help you stay focused on growth while we handle the numbers.
