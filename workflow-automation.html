Who usually needs this

Is this the right
fit for your business?

This capability is best suited for businesses at a specific stage or facing a specific set of challenges. See if any of these describe where you are right now.

Teams scaling faster than process maturity
When the business is growing quickly but the operational processes that support it were designed for a much smaller team, automation closes the gap without adding headcount.
Operations with frequent handoffs between people or systems
Every handoff is a point of failure. When work crosses teams, tools, or approval layers, automation enforces consistency that people alone cannot sustain.
Businesses facing recurring execution errors
If the same types of mistakes appear repeatedly despite training and good intentions, the process itself is the problem. Automation removes the dependency on error-free manual execution.
